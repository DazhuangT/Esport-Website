More Than Just Video Game!

In the past, people usually treated video only as an entertainment tool. However, after years of development, E-sports jump on the table.

E-sports stand for electronic sports, it is a form of competition using video games.

Today, E-sports has become a serious sport event. Video games being played professionally at a highly competitive level. In the same way as you have pros in things like Basketball, there are now pros in a variety of esports like League of Legends, Counter-Strike, Dota 2, and Fortnite. What began as an industry in back rooms and crowded auditoriums now fills stadiums worldwide, with top players making six figures in most of the tier-1 games.

Moreover, followed by the step of game elders like Go and Chess. E-sports has became the offical sports event in Asian Games 2022 (The Olympics in Asia). Some E-sports Athletes can actually win back a metal for their country by playing video games.

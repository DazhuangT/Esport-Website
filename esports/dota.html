The International DOTA2 Championships

Dota 2 is a MOBA type game (Multiplayer Online Battle Arena).

The way to play this type of game is: generally you need to buy equipment in battle. Players are usually divided into two teams. The two teams compete with each other on a scattered game map. Each player controls the selected character through an RTS-style interface.

MOBA was once called as dota-like game, because dota was the first hit game of this type of game. The moba game after dota always contains some elements that dota invented.

  

The International DOTA2 Championships, also known as TI, founded in 2011, is a global E-sports event, held annually by Valve Corporation. The trophy is a special champion shield of Valve Corporation called Aegis of Champions. Each championship team will be recorded in the game fountain Aegis of Champions.

TI is keeping the record of the highest reward prize money in the E-sports history.

TI5’s total prize money of tens of millions of dollars has made dota2 reach the peak of public opinion, and TI6’s total prize money of more than 20 million US dollars, the impact is ahead, and the champion alone can monopolize more than 9 million US dollars in prize money.
